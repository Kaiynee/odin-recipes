Fries

Description

French fries are long, thin pieces of potato fried in oil or fat. The French fries were thin and crispy. To cook the French fries, put them into a deep pan of oil.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash potatoes
  2. Oil up pan
  3. Put fires in pan
  4. Add salt
  5. Take out when cripsy and brown on edges
